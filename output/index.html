ASF - Data Privacy

Welcome to the Data Privacy website.

The Apache Software Foundation is committed to protect the privacy of their Members, Committers and Users.

Privacy related questions

If you have questions related to privacy at The Apache Software Foundation which are not of confidential nature, feel free to ask at our (archived) mailing list:

privacy@apache.org

If you have confidential questions, please reach out to V.P., Data Privacy directly:

vp-privacy@apache.org

Privacy complaints / Removal requests

If you have complaints aligning with the GDPR or CCPA, please reach out to:

vp-privacy@apache.org
